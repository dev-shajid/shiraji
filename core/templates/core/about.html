ABOUT SHIRAJI

Learn about our company and our mission

Our History

Shiraji General Contracting was established in Abu Dhabi with a vision to deliver exceptional construction services to clients throughout the United Arab Emirates.

Since our founding, we have grown into a trusted name in the construction industry, known for our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Our team of experienced professionals brings decades of combined experience to every project, ensuring that we deliver results that exceed expectations.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional construction services that transform our clients' visions into reality, while maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and customer satisfaction.

We are committed to:

  • Excellence in every aspect of our work
  • Building lasting relationships with our clients
  • Creating safe and sustainable structures
  • Contributing positively to the communities we serve

Our Vision

To be the leading construction company in the UAE, recognized for our innovative solutions, exceptional quality, and unwavering commitment to client satisfaction.

We aspire to:

  • Set new standards in the construction industry
  • Embrace innovative technologies and sustainable practices
  • Develop our team to reach their full potential
  • Expand our services while maintaining our core values
